Our Story

Born from Passion, Driven by Purpose

Founded in Nakuru, Kenya by Kelly Johns, June Chepkemoi, and Deborah Blessings, Veritas KE was born from a shared passion: to uplift startups through smart, beautiful, and performance-driven digital solutions.

We saw a gap in accessible tech for early-stage founders — and we're here to close it. Our vision started with helping startups gain an online presence, but as we journeyed alongside them, we realized they needed more than just a website.

That's why we've expanded our services to cover business legality, branding, and documentation essentials — everything a startup needs to build a solid foundation.
