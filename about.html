Our Story

Founded in Nakuru, Kenya by Kelly Johns, June Chepkemoi, and Deborah Blessings, Veritas KE was born from a shared passion: to uplift startups through smart, beautiful, and performance-driven digital solutions. We saw a gap in accessible tech for early-stage founders — and we're here to close it.

Our vision started with helping startups gain an online presence. But as we journeyed alongside them, we realized they needed more than just a website — they needed support for the entire foundation of their business. That’s why we’ve expanded our services to also cover business legality, branding, and documentation essentials.
